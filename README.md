## Case Studies

### Case 1: Stabilizing Enterprise Knowledge Systems in a Regulated Environment

**Context**  
A large organization maintained hundreds of fragmented knowledge artifacts across teams, creating inconsistent guidance, operational risk, and poor searchability.

**Problem**  
Documentation debt slowed delivery, increased support escalations, and introduced compliance risk due to outdated or conflicting content.

**My Role**  
Product Owner responsible for documentation strategy, backlog prioritization, stakeholder alignment, and delivery readiness.

**Actions**  
- Audited and rationalized existing knowledge assets across multiple departments  
- Defined standardized article structures and governance rules  
- Prioritized remediation work in a shared backlog aligned to operational risk  
- Partnered with engineering and business teams to align content with system behavior  
- Established review and maintenance workflows to prevent regression

**Outcome**  
- Improved content consistency and searchability  
- Reduced delivery friction for downstream teams  
- Lowered operational and compliance risk tied to outdated documentation

### Case 2: Bridging Product, Documentation, and Delivery During Platform Transformation

**Context**  
An enterprise platform initiative introduced new tools and workflows impacting multiple stakeholder groups.

**Problem**  
Requirements were fragmented, documentation lagged delivery, and teams struggled to adopt changes consistently.

**My Role**  
Product Owner acting as the bridge between product, documentation, engineering, and operations.

**Actions**  
- Clarified scope and success criteria with business and technical stakeholders  
- Converted high-level requirements into actionable backlog items  
- Aligned documentation deliverables with sprint execution  
- Facilitated cross-team prioritization and dependency management  
- Provided delivery readiness support ahead of release

**Outcome**  
- Improved stakeholder alignment and delivery predictability  
- Increased adoption through clearer guidance and communication  
- Reduced post-release support issues
