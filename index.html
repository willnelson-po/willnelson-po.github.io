Case Studies

Stabilizing Enterprise Knowledge Systems in a Regulated Environment

Context
A large organization maintained hundreds of fragmented knowledge artifacts across teams, creating inconsistent guidance, operational risk, and poor searchability.

Problem
Documentation debt slowed delivery, increased support escalations, and introduced compliance risk due to outdated or conflicting content.

My Role
Product Owner responsible for documentation strategy, backlog prioritization, stakeholder alignment, and delivery readiness.

Actions

  • Audited and rationalized existing knowledge assets across multiple departments
  • Defined standardized article structures and governance rules
  • Prioritized remediation work aligned to operational risk
  • Partnered with engineering and business teams to align content with system behavior
  • Established review and maintenance workflows to prevent regression

Outcome
Improved consistency, searchability, and reduced operational and compliance risk.
